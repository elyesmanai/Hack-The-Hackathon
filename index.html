So What is a Hackathon?

"Hack" + "Marathon"

Between 12 and 48 hours to make a working project and present it

Why Should I hackathon?

  • Test Your Technical Skills
  • Test Your Presentation Skills
  • Check your level with others
  • Make new friends
  • Network
  • Learn something new
  • Have fun !
  • Test your Thinking Skills
  • Test your work under Pressure Skills
  • Test your teamwork Skills
  • Know better the persons you chose as your team
  • It's a good thing to add to your CV

How do I find Hackathons?

Ask your contacts for present or later opportunities
